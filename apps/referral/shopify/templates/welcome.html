Invite for a Gift

Invite for a Gift is Shopify App to help your store generate more sales by using your customers' social networks.

How it Works

When your customers complete a purchase, they'll be prompted to refer their friends for a gift.

When their friends shop on your site, they'll be prompted to complete a purchase so that they can get their gift.

Once the friends have made a purchase, they'll be invited to give gifts to their friends and the cycle continues!

We'll add a note to your orders to let you know when to include the gift - so be sure to watch for it when fulfilling your orders!

The Gift

As for the gift itself, start thinking of the perfect gift for your buyers! Should it be a limited edition item or overstock from last season? Pick something you know your buyers will love!

The cooler the gift, the more your customers will talk about it on their social networks!
