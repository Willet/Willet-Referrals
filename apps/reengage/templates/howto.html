Overview

Engage puts you in control of how you reach out to users who liked, tweeted, pinned, or emailed your product.

To learn about how Engage works for the different social networks, take a look at the navigation to the left.

**Soon you'll be able to reach out to users who did social actions with specific categories or products!

Facebook

To reach out to customers on Facebook, start by creating posts to go in your queue. Posts are sent out at regular intervals to people who have liked one of your products. You can find out when post are scheduled by looking in the top-right of the queue area.

To reach out to your customers

1. Click the 'New Post' button.

2. Give your post a title. The title you give your post will only be visible to you.

3. If this is not your first post, you can decide to schedule your post at the beginning of the queue (as soon as possible) or the end of the queue (as late as possible).

4. Add content to your post. This content will be what your customers will see on their Facebook feed when the post is sent.

**Soon you'll be able to embed links, images, and videos in your Facebook posts as well!
