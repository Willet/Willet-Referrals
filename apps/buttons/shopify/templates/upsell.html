Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do you determine which social networks a customer uses?

    The largest social networks feature passive ways to determine if someone is a user, without revealing anything about that person. In addition, we make a note whenever someone shares with a network.

  • What if you don't find any networks they are using?

    Smart ShopConnection always displays 3 social buttons. We will default to the 3 that you choose. we use Pinterest, Tumblr, and the Fancy if you haven't set a default.

  • Are there any privacy concerns with this?

    Nope. We don't determine any personal information, and everything happens on your customers' computer. It is never shared or stored elsewhere.
